## Context

_Describe the strategic situation driving this decision._
Explain the product, market, organizational, or technical forces at play. What is changing, what
tension exists, or what commitment is being made explicit? Include constraints, stakeholder inputs,
or prior strategy that frames the choice. Future readers should understand *why this mattered* at
the time.

## Direction

_State the strategic commitment clearly._
Describe what the team/product is committing to and — equally important — what it is ruling out.
A good direction is actionable: teams should be able to use it to make downstream decisions without
re-litigating this one. Avoid vague aspiration; be specific enough to resolve trade-offs.

## Rationale

_Explain why this direction over the alternatives._
Name the alternatives seriously considered. For each, explain why it was not chosen. Acknowledge
the trade-offs accepted. This section is what separates a decision from a decree — it shows the
reasoning so future teams can judge whether the context has changed enough to revisit.
