# Decision Records Skill

Follow **`SKILL.md`** in this same directory (for example `.cursor/skills/dev/decision-records/SKILL.md` when the skill is installed). It is the single source of truth for:

- Architecture Decision Records (ADR), Design Decision Records (DDR), and Strategic Design Records (SDR)
- Shorthand commands: `/decision a`, `/decision d`, `/decision s`
- Folder conventions, templates under `assets/`, and eval scenarios under `evals/`

Use `SKILL.md` for full workflows; do not duplicate its content here.
